SpringApplication的prepareEnvironment() 方法会进行Environment的创建,具体在getOrCreateEnvironment方法中,而且会根据
webApplicationType的类型来创建

StandardServletEnvironment 继承 StandardEnvironment
还是StandardReactiveWebEnvironment 继承 StandardEnvironment
还是StandardEnvironment

StandardEnvironment会加载jvm系统信息

然后创建ApplicationEnvironmentPreparedEvent事件并通知各个ApplicationListener进行处理
