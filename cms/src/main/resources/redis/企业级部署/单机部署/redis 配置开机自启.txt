3: 配置开机自启服务

    vi /lib/systemd/system/redis.service 配置如下内容

    [Unit]

    Description=redis-server

    After=network.target


    [Service]

    Type=forking

    ExecStart=/usr/local/bin/redis-server /usr/local/redis/redis.conf

    ExecReload=

    ExecStop=/usr/local/bin/redis-cli -h 192.168.109.130 -p 6379 -a test1234 shutdown

    PrivateTmp=true


    [Install]

    WantedBy=multi-user.target

4： 重启systemctl
    systemctl daemon-reload

5：设置开机自启

    systemctl enable redis.service
