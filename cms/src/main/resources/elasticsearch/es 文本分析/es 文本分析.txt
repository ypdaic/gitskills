文本分析包括下面3个部分

1：字符过滤(char filter)

    如：                                 分析后变成这样
     I like ELK, it include Elas&kib&log --------------->  I like ELK, it include Elas and kib and log
2：字符分词
    分词 token 标准分词
     如：
    I like ELK, it include Elas and kib and log 分词为 I   like   ELK  it  include Elas   and   kib   and  log
3：分词过滤

    小写的分词过滤器 （token filter）
    如：
     I   like   ELK  it  include Elas   and   kib   and  log 过滤后  I like elk it inculde elas kib log
