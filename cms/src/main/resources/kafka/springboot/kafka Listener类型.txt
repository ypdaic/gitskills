其实也就是3种接口类型：
    非批量：
    AcknowledgingMessageListener 可以获取Acknowledgment
    ConsumerAwareMessageListener 可以获取Consumer
    AcknowledgingConsumerAwareMessageListener 可以获取Acknowledgment，Consumer

    批量和非批量一样：
    BatchAcknowledgingMessageListener
    BatchConsumerAwareMessageListener
    BatchAcknowledgingConsumerAwareMessageListener
