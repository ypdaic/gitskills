最终调到Client的实现Default，ApacheHttpClient，OkHttpClient进行具体的请求

ApacheHttpClient，OkHttpClient这两个通过配置项feign.httpclient.enabled = true 或者feign.okhttp.enable=true开启，
默认使用Default这个实现
OkHttpClient需要引入如下依赖：

        <dependency>
            <groupId>io.github.openfeign</groupId>
            <artifactId>feign-okhttp</artifactId>
        </dependency>


实际上LoadBalancerFeignClient包装了ApacheHttpClient，OkHttpClient，Client

对SynchronousMethodHandler暴露的其实是LoadBalancerFeignClient
注入的代码位置：FeignClientFactoryBean  protected <T> T loadBalance(Feign.Builder builder, FeignContext context,
                                			HardCodedTarget<T> target)
