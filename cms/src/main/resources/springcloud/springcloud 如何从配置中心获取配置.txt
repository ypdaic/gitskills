首先通过spring.cloud.context  模块的spring.factories 文件配置了如下4个config

org.springframework.cloud.bootstrap.BootstrapConfiguration=\
org.springframework.cloud.bootstrap.config.PropertySourceBootstrapConfiguration,\
org.springframework.cloud.bootstrap.encrypt.EncryptionBootstrapConfiguration,\
org.springframework.cloud.autoconfigure.ConfigurationPropertiesRebinderAutoConfiguration,\
org.springframework.boot.autoconfigure.context.PropertyPlaceholderAutoConfiguration

PropertySourceBootstrapConfiguration  就是用来从配置中心获取配置的

PropertySourceBootstrapConfiguration获取配置就是通过PropertySourceLocator的实现类获取

PropertySourceLocator的实现类对于不同的配置中心有不同的实现

具体的实现如下：
NacosPropertySourceLocator (org.springframework.cloud.alibaba.nacos.client)
EnvironmentRepositoryPropertySourceLocator (org.springframework.cloud.config.server.environment)
ZookeeperPropertySourceLocator (org.springframework.cloud.zookeeper.config)
ConsulPropertySourceLocator (org.springframework.cloud.consul.config)
ConfigServicePropertySourceLocator (org.springframework.cloud.config.client)
